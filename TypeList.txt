#en-US:

build: Set block installation and block destruction.

tnt: Whether the surrounding block is destroyed (including the frame)

invincible: It means invincible, except (/kill, physical strength).

lava-flow: Whether to let the lava flow or not

water: Whether to let the water flow or not

use: Whether players can't click when they click anywhere

tnt-Damage: Decide whether to be damaged by the explosion or not

pvp: Determine if you want to prevent players from fighting between them

chat: You cannot type chat except for an in-zone administrator.

Mob-damage: It will not be damaged by the mob.

Mob-pvp: Unable to hit all entities except entities (except players)

fire: Players don't burn when they enter the area!
